Personality  
*Called a weed but still blooms.*  
  
**Emotionally Reserved**  
often not/rarely being the person who initiates conversations to associates he isn't known with, something like he doesn't need to communicate and people somehow understands what he needs or is trying to say. He doesn't mind pestering or annoying things and rarely ever cares or gets mad, although he still replies to them when needed or when talked to. Yeosin struggles to show emotion or affection, reserved from his childhood Yeosin most likely wouldn't show it publicly. He tends to show his care to people through other ways. Yeosin is easy and hard to talk to at the same time, on the outside showing he has no interest but inside he is easygoing.  

**Ambitious and Courages**  
Once Yeosin feels avid/driven onto something he will not back down to get to know more about that person or object. Yeosin is easily driven by his interests, often leaving other things for it, he looks up at the interest as something aspiring and worth his time. Though he is not that reckless, Yeosin will not stop in getting to know the person/object by approaching, getting to know, initiating more conversations, and treating them by their boundaries and likes. Without Yeosin's interest in something, he won't put much thought into it.  

**Reciprocal and Curious**  
When treated nicely, Yeosin returns that kindness by replying through action, if not much kindness is displayed, he won't set a wall easily, though they won't seem very interested; they aren't that mean and inattentive of a person. Yeosin would still try to keep up a conversation and confront them of their traits trying to get to know them if they seem to keep. Rarely checks up daily but still shows support once in a while.  

**Yeosin, Is Two faced. do not be tricked.**  
Yeosin keeps secrets, thoughts no one knows, he might look like he's comfortable with you outside. But Yeosin? Two colors, two wings, two looks, and two faces.

 **Lore**  
  
*Before curse*  
  
Yeosin's beautiful pale white skin was always similar before and after the curse. Hair soft and silver, skin smooth and pale. His mother cherished her, and she forever will to her **daughter**. Yeosin's mother wanted a daughter as a child, but unfortunately, she got Yeosin instead, a son.  

 That didn't stop her, she manipulated Yeosin to wear dresses, long hair, bows, feminine things, and especially maintain his looks. Yeosin was seen like some goddess over her mother's manipulative intentions but at the same time treated horribly.   

Yeosin was often taking diets, getting starved, or being forced to vomit out food to maintain his weight. Other than that she made her gain a feminine physical body using colors resembling a female. This made Yeosin weary of his existence and chose not to care over anything too much and just follow, or you could call Apathetic.  

*After curse*  

Because of the wound created on his mental barrier, Yeosin of course didn't really give much care into the curse set upon him. He caught the curse from the witch's craft, her being lonely at night only gave the witch an easier opportunity to curse him, who knew the witch would find a person by the lake near a field of dandelions? He was a bit more glad that he had received the curse, he could finally be a new person.   
 
Thinking of freedom that bestowed upon him with relief. Yet that did not sink well, being just slightly different from his original appearance, this was the curse. He couldn't escape the title of a fake goddess.  

Through this he convinced himself he was just somewhere in between leading to a non-binary. The present time is now. Not caring about much anymore, just there to relax and rest. He's safe now isn't he?
